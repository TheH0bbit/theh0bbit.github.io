Project Overview

Harmony of the Stars is a story-driven RPG and Visual Novel hybrid developed entirely as a solo project over approximately 1.5 years.


The project combines narrative content, relationship systems, exploration, RPG progression, turn-based combat, inventory management, world navigation, character scheduling, and quest systems within a customized Ren'Py framework.


In addition to software development, the project involved worldbuilding, narrative design, UI creation, image editing, animation production, map creation, content integration, release management, and ongoing maintenance after launch.


Harmony of the Stars is intended for adult audiences and contains mature content. This portfolio focuses on the technical, design, and development aspects of the project.

Technical Systems

A collection of interconnected gameplay systems designed to extend the capabilities of Ren'Py beyond traditional visual novel mechanics. The project emphasizes modular architecture, maintainability, data-driven content, and save compatibility for future updates.

Turn-Based Combat System

Custom combat framework featuring initiative-based turn order, status effects, damage calculations, enemy AI, equipment integration, elemental affinities, and skill interactions.

Inventory & Equipment Framework

Inventory architecture supporting equipment management, item categorization, character statistics, consumables, progression systems, and inventory persistence.

Quest Management System

Dynamic quest framework supporting objectives, rewards, progression states, hint systems, event integration, and narrative progression.

Relationship Progression

Character relationship architecture supporting affection tracking, progression milestones, unlockable content, event triggers, and long-term character development.

Skill Tree Framework

Character-specific progression trees supporting unlockable abilities, branching development paths, and RPG-style long-term progression.

World & Navigation

Exploration is structured across multiple navigation layers, allowing players to move between regions, cities, locations, dungeons, events, and character interactions while maintaining progression and world state.

Regional Travel System

Large-scale exploration layer connecting major regions, story progression paths, discoverable locations, and future content expansions.

Interactive Town Navigation

City exploration framework used for events, quests, character encounters, progression tracking, and world interaction.

Location Navigation Framework

Fast-travel and location access system supporting player activities, story events, exploration, and character scheduling.

Narrative Design

Beyond gameplay systems, Harmony of the Stars features an original fantasy setting, multiple story arcs, character relationships, side content, and long-term narrative progression integrated directly into gameplay systems.

Story-Driven Progression

Narrative events are closely integrated with quest progression, exploration, relationship development, and gameplay systems. Story choices and progression unlock new locations, characters, and content.

Worldbuilding & Content Creation

In addition to software development, the project required extensive content production including worldbuilding, map creation, visual design, animation production, image editing, user interface design, and content integration.

Visual Identity & Presentation

Design and implementation of the game's visual presentation, user interface styling, branding, menus, and overall player experience.

Worldbuilding

Creation of an original fantasy setting including nations, locations, lore, history, characters, progression structure, and narrative framework.
