Project Overview

Harmony of the Stars is a story-driven RPG and Visual Novel hybrid developed entirely as a solo project.


The project combines narrative content, relationship systems, exploration, RPG progression, turn-based combat, inventory management, world navigation, and quest systems within a customized Ren'Py framework.


Beyond programming, the project also involved worldbuilding, narrative design, UI creation, image editing, animation production, map creation, and content integration.

Technical Systems

A collection of interconnected gameplay systems designed to transform a traditional visual novel engine into a fully featured RPG framework.

Turn-Based Combat

Initiative-based combat framework supporting skills, equipment, damage calculations, status effects, elemental interactions, and party management.

Inventory & Equipment

Custom inventory system featuring equipment slots, item categories, stat integration, consumables, progression tracking, and character management.

Quest Framework

Dynamic quest tracking system with objectives, rewards, progression states, hints, and character integration.

Relationship System

Character progression system managing affection, unlockable content, relationship levels, and event progression.

Skill Trees

Character-specific progression trees supporting unlockable abilities and long-term RPG growth.

World & Navigation

Multiple exploration layers allow the player to navigate between regions, cities, story locations, and character events.

Regional Travel

Large-scale exploration layer connecting major areas and progression routes.

City Exploration

Interactive city navigation system used for quests, events, character encounters, and progression.

Location Navigation

Fast navigation framework allowing access to locations, activities, and story content.

Narrative & Content

The game features an original fantasy setting, multiple story arcs, character relationships, side content, and a growing cast of characters integrated directly into gameplay systems.

Story-Driven Progression

Narrative content drives player progression while interacting with quests, exploration, combat, and character systems.

Visual Design & Worldbuilding

In addition to programming, the project required the creation of maps, worldbuilding documentation, visual assets, animations, interface design, and environmental presentation.

Main Menu & Presentation

Custom visual identity and user interface design supporting the game's fantasy setting.

Worldbuilding

Original world map, nations, locations, lore, and narrative structure created specifically for the game.
